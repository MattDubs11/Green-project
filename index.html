Montefiore Square Reimagined

A greener Harlem plaza with plants, seating, and wellness spaces

About the Project

This redesign transforms Montefiore Square into a greener and more welcoming pedestrian plaza inspired by Harlem community life.

The Problem

Lack of Greenery

The current plaza lacks plants, shade, and comfortable seating.

Weak Community Use

The space needs flexible programming for events and local gathering.

Safety + Comfort

Better visibility, crossings, and seating improve comfort and access.

Final Proposal

The final design introduces lush planting, curved benches, open lawn space, and better circulation.
