Montefiore Square Reimagined

A greener Harlem plaza with plants, seating, and wellness spaces

About the Project

This redesign transforms Montefiore Square into a greener and more welcoming pedestrian plaza inspired by Harlem community life, improved safety, and stronger public gathering spaces.

Design Process: Before → During → After

Before

The existing plaza has limited greenery, little seating, and weak spatial identity.

During

Early redesign studies explored better circulation, market activity, and green space integration.

After

The final proposal creates a vibrant Harlem plaza with planting, seating, lighting, and community use.

Final Proposal

The final design introduces lush planting, curved benches, open lawn space, safer pedestrian circulation, and flexible community market programming.
